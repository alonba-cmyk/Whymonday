End-to-end segmented funnels — design exploration

Three directions, six representative slides

Same content, three different visual languages — each shown at desktop (1280×720) and mobile (390×844) size. Pick a direction below and we'll apply it across all 24 slides.

A — Refined Dark
The current direction, taken further: deeper elevation, gradient accents, glow, premium startup-pitch feel.
B — Monday Light
White canvas, editorial black type, monday.com brand colors (purple/green/orange/red) as accents. Boardroom-daylight feel.
C — Bold Board
Light gray canvas + white cards echo monday.com's own product UI — colored group bars, solid status pills, dashboard widgets.
