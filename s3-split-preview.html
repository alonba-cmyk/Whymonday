Slide 1 — The Gap (קיים, לא נוגעים)

The Gap

But most businesses can't see the impact of people and agents working together on the bottom line.

90%
of employees use personal AI tools at work
yet
5%
of enterprise AI pilots deliver measurable business impact

MIT Sloan Management Review

What's standing in their way?

Siloed Agents
Most people use AI as individuals.
Outputs stay personal and never scale.
Lack of Context
When agents don't know enough about the business,
they produce generic outputs, not real action.
Outside the Workflow
Agents live in a chat window, not where the work is done.
Every handoff is a gap where execution breaks down.
